# Senior AI Engineer

**Location:** Pune, Noida, Hyderabad

**Experience Required:** 5-9 years

## About the role

We are hiring a Senior AI Engineer to lead the next generation of our
retrieval and ranking platform. You'll own end-to-end production
pipelines — from embeddings and vector search to ranking evaluation and
A/B testing.

## Responsibilities

- Build production-grade embedding-based retrieval systems
- Operate hybrid search stacks (sparse + dense) with sub-200ms latency
- Drive ranking quality through NDCG / MRR / MAP and offline-to-online correlation
- Lead A/B test design and analysis for ranking experiments
- Mentor mid-level engineers across IR, NLP, and MLOps
- Collaborate cross-functionally with product, design, and platform teams

## Things you absolutely need

- Production embeddings-based retrieval (sentence-transformers, BGE, E5)
- Vector databases (Pinecone, Weaviate, Qdrant, Milvus, FAISS)
- Strong Python and PyTorch
- Ranking system evaluation (NDCG, MRR, MAP, A/B testing)

## Things we'd like you to have

- LLM fine-tuning (LoRA, QLoRA, PEFT)
- Learning-to-rank (XGBoost-based or neural)
- HR-tech or marketplace product experience
- Open-source contributions in AI/ML

## Qualifications

- Master's degree in Computer Science, Machine Learning, or related field
- 5+ years of applied ML production experience
- Excellent communication and stakeholder management

## Things we explicitly do NOT want

- Pure research-only background with no production deployment
- AI experience limited to LangChain + hosted-LLM usage
- Senior engineers who haven't shipped code in 18+ months
